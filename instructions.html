How do you play wordle?

  • Wordle is simple.
  • Type in a word as a guess, and the game tells you which letters are and aren't in the word.
  • Type in one letter in each box and click on the Check button to verify if you have guessed the word correctly.
  • When you guess a word, the letter tile will change colour to green, orange or grey, which all mean different things.
  • If the letter is highlighted as green, that means the letter is in the correct place.
  • If any of the letters is highlighted as orange, that means the letter in the wrong place.
  • A grey tile means the letter isn't in the word at all. So you might want to exclude this letter in your next attempt.
