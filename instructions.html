How do you play wordle?

  • Wordle is simple.

  • Type in a word as a guess, and the game tells you which letters are or aren't in the word.

  • When you guess a word, the letter tile will change colour to green, yellow or grey,which all mean different things.

  • Green: The letter is right and in the correct position in the word.

  • If any of the letters is highlighted as yellow, that means that that letter is in the word, but it's in the wrong place.

  • A grey tile means the letter isn't in the word at all.
